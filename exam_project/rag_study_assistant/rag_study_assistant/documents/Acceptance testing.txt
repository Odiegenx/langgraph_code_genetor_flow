Types of Acceptance tests:

• User Acceptance Testing (UAT)
• Operational Acceptance Testing
• Validation of the products’ operations in a real situation
• Contract and Regulations Testing
• Legal bonds, safety standards (banking, pharmaceutical),
governmental regulations (defense)
• Alpha Testing. In-house
• Beta Testing. At the customer’s premises
• Pilot phase. Product release
on a limited geographical or logical scale

UAT - User acceptance tests
	a test from the perspective of the users and other stateholders
	not just a tests of the software, nor of the functionality, performance, reliability and security nor of the usability of the system
	the nearest we get to "the real thing" without actually taking the risk of releasing the system into service.
